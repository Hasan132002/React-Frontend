    UTILITY

    Bringing NFTs out of its box of just being unique digital assets, we grant holders privileges, rights, and rewards that tie in directly with the entertainment industry. From on-chain voting to one day walking  the red-carpet, Dream3 gives owners the full experience of film-making that transcends the digital world and encompasses IRL experiences.

    SCALABILITY

    Storylines can scale infinitely as long as the community sees fit (i.e  Fast & Furious, Star Wars etc). Starting with short form content, the ceiling is infinite as the content can evolve towards streaming series, theatrical releases, or even storyline gaming. Due to Dream3's story centric and entertainment driven foundation, we're able to help bring Web3 media towards more mainstream avenues of entertainment distribution.

    DIGITAL RENNAISANCE

    Filmmaking & animation brings forth an all inclusive environment that allows NFT projects to thrive within the same ecosystem. By incorporating NFTs into storytelling we're pushing forward the culture of web3 towards mainstream media. We will be featuring a variety of projects and incorporate them in our lores to bring together. 

    LONG TERM GOAL

    Using NFTs to build the foundation of interactive media. 

    On-chain voting allows viewers to exercise they're creativity and feel more connected with the content they consume. Introducing token verification to streaming platforms will also give users a breath of fresh air as they'll be re-warded with a digital asset upon their choice in crucial moments of how the story turns. Gamifying content through NFTs is the first step towards interactive media.
